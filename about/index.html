OUR MISION

With an aim to strive towards promoting entrepreneurship, we at eDC IIT Delhi are a group of enthusiasts working continuously to provide a platform to foster, inspire and help the young aspiring students open the door of the promising & exciting world of entrepreneurship

OUR VISION

The Entrepreneurship Development Cell of IIT Delhi was set up in January 2007 with a vision to promote and nurture Entrepreneurship amongst the student community and help aspiring student entrepreneurs achive their entrepreneurial aspirations. We strongly believe that entrepreneurship holds the potential to transform not just our country but the entire world. As an institute, we are proud to have many luminary alumni who have made their mark in the entrepreneurial sphere. We are continuously working towards facilitating entrepreneurship education and develop a comprehensive resource pool. In this pursuit, eDC IIT Delhi has successfully organized a plethora of events ranging from Exclusive Townhall Sessions with top entrepreneurs to competitions, workshops and colloquiums.
