Register

To register for BECon 2020, all the interested students can fill the form given above. Based on the responses, the top 400 delegates would get the chance to avail this priceless experience.

Accommodation will also be provided, the details of which will be sent to you once you are selected for the conference.

(Note that accommodation is not compulsory for all and delegates will have to pay later on for accommodation). Certificates will be provided for attending events and workshops (No extra fee will be charged for attending the workshops during BECON)
