About

IIT Delhi brings together the most happening and innovative startups, along with C-level executives from the top corporations, incubator investors, HNWIS, VC funds, banks, students and technology companies.

The event sets the stage for a curated set of startups to present themselves in front of an audience comprising of investors, early-stage adopters, mentors, potential co-founders and partners. Technology companies will be able to showcase their latest technologies which would help in the growth of startups.

The one-day event will feature thought leaders, experienced entrepreneurs and investors taking part in keynotes, panel discussions and interactive sessions.

Important highlights about Expo

  • International keynotes
  • Investor’s Lounge
  • Experts workshops
  • Pitch Competition
  • Corporate Innovation launch
  • Networking and Parties

  1. Pitching Opportunities Both Personalised and Combined pitching sessions
